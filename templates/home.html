    •   Available Courses in Department of (CCS), Computer Science


    Computer Science
    Introduction to Computer Science

    Explore the fundamental concepts of computer science in this introductory course. Learn about algorithms, data structures, programming languages, and more. Whether you're new to the world of coding or looking to build a strong foundation, this course is the perfect starting point for your computer science journey.

    Computer Network Administration
    Introduction to Network Administration

    Introduction to Network Administration is a fundamental course that provides an overview of key concepts and practices in managing computer networks. Students will learn the basics of network setup, configuration, and troubleshooting, as well as gain insights into network security and administration best practices. This course is an excellent starting point for those looking to pursue a career in network administration or related fields.

    Computer Science Application Development
    Introduction to Application Development

    Explore the fundamentals of application development in this comprehensive course. Learn the essential programming languages, tools, and techniques to kickstart your journey as an app developer. Whether you're interested in web or mobile app development, this course provides the knowledge and skills you need to get started. Dive into the world of coding, design, and user experience to create innovative and user-friendly applications.

    Computer Science Social Computing
    Introduction to Social Computing

    Explore the dynamic world of social computing with our introductory course. Gain insights into the impact of social media, online communities, and collaborative technologies on society. Learn how these digital platforms are shaping communication, information sharing, and social interactions. Dive into the fundamentals of social computing and its applications, equipping yourself with the skills to thrive in the digital age.

    Computational & Data Sciences
    What is Computational & Data Sciences

    Computational & Data Sciences is an interdisciplinary field that explores the power of computational methods and data analysis in solving complex problems. This course introduces students to the fundamental principles of data science, computer programming, and statistical analysis. Through hands-on projects and real-world applications, students gain the skills to extract valuable insights from data, make data-driven decisions, and contribute to advancements in various domains, from business and healthcare to scientific research and beyond.

    Information & Network Security
    Does Information & Network Security Accurate?

    Learn the core principles of securing information and networks, and gain the skills to protect digital assets against cyber threats. Explore the accuracy and reliability of security measures in this comprehensive course.

    Explore the principles and practices of safeguarding digital information and networks. Learn about encryption, firewall technologies, threat detection, and security best practices to protect against cyber threats and vulnerabilities. Gain essential skills to secure data and maintain the integrity of network systems.
